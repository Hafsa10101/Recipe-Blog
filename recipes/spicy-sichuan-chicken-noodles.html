Spicy Sichuan Chicken Noodles

Description

These Spicy Sichuan Chicken Noodles bring the authentic heat of Sichuan cuisine right to your kitchen. With tender chicken slices, aromatic garlic, ginger, and Sichuan peppercorns, this dish is a fiery treat for spice lovers looking for a hearty and satisfying meal.

Ingredients

  • 200g boneless chicken breast, thinly sliced
  • 200g noodles (your choice)
  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • 4-5 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon ginger, minced
  • 2 tablespoons Sichuan peppercorns
  • 2-3 dried red chilies, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon chili paste (adjust to taste)
  • 1 tablespoon vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • Salt to taste
  • Spring onions, chopped (for garnish)

Steps

  1. Cook the noodles according to package instructions, drain, and set aside.
  2. Heat 1 tablespoon oil in a pan, add the chicken slices, and stir-fry until cooked through. Remove and set aside.
  3. In the same pan, add the remaining oil. Saut√© the garlic, ginger, Sichuan peppercorns, and dried red chilies until fragrant.
  4. Add the soy sauce, chili paste, vinegar, sugar, and salt. Stir well.
  5. Add the cooked chicken and noodles to the pan and toss everything together.
  6. Garnish with chopped spring onions and serve hot.
