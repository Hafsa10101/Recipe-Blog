Veg Singaporean Hakka Noodles

Description

A colorful and flavorful vegetarian dish, Veg Singaporean Hakka Noodles is a delightful combination of crisp veggies and perfectly cooked Hakka noodles, tossed in a blend of soy sauce, chili sauce, and curry powder. It’s a vibrant dish that’s both delicious and wholesome, ideal for a quick weeknight dinner.

Ingredients

  • 200g Hakka noodles
  • 1 tablespoon oil
  • 3-4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 small onion, sliced
  • 1 small carrot, julienned
  • 1 small capsicum, julienned
  • 1/2 cup cabbage, shredded
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon chili sauce
  • 1 tablespoon tomato ketchup
  • 1 teaspoon vinegar
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon curry powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Spring onions, chopped (for garnish)

Steps

  1. Cook the Hakka noodles according to package instructions, drain, and set aside.
  2. Heat oil in a pan, sauté the minced garlic until golden.
  3. Add the sliced onion and sauté until translucent.
  4. Add the carrot, capsicum, and cabbage, and stir-fry for 2-3 minutes until slightly tender but still crisp.
  5. Stir in the soy sauce, chili sauce, tomato ketchup, vinegar, turmeric powder, curry powder, and salt.
  6. Add the cooked noodles and toss everything together until well combined.
  7. Garnish with chopped spring onions and serve hot.
